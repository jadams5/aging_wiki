# Integumentary System

Navigational overlay for skin and its appendages. Skin is the most visible aging organ and a uniquely accessible window on aging biology: it combines intrinsic (chronological) aging with photoaging (UV-driven), making it a natural testbed for interventions ([[retinoids]], procedural resurfacing). The dermal-epidermal compartment is rich in stem-cell, senescence, and extracellular-matrix biology.

## Tissues & organs

- [[skin]] — the organ; intrinsic vs photoaging
- [[epidermis]] — outer epithelial layer; barrier function, keratinocyte turnover
- [[dermis]] — connective layer; collagen/elastin matrix, the main site of wrinkle/laxity biology

## Cell types

- [[keratinocytes]] — epidermal barrier cells
- [[melanocytes]] · [[melanocyte-stem-cells]] — pigment cells; exhaustion drives [[hair-greying]]
- [[langerhans-cells]] — epidermal-resident immune sentinels (overlap with [[immune-system]])
- [[dermal-fibroblasts]] — ECM-producing; senescence/SASP drives dermal thinning

## Aging phenotypes

- [[skin-aging]] — the integrative phenotype (wrinkles, laxity, atrophy)
- [[androgenetic-alopecia]] — patterned hair loss
- [[hair-greying]] — melanocyte-stem-cell exhaustion
- [[cherry-angioma]] — benign vascular proliferation of aging skin

## Key interventions & axes

- [[retinoids]] (class), [[tretinoin]], [[tazarotene]], [[bakuchiol]] — collagen-stimulating topicals
- Procedural: [[dermatologic-resurfacing]], [[microneedling]], [[chemical-peels]]
- Injectable: [[injectable-skin-boosters]] (class), [[pdrn]], [[biostimulatory-fillers]], [[exosome-skin-therapy]] — intradermal biostimulators / fillers
- ECM: [[col1a1|collagen I]], [[mmp-1]]; [[uv-protection]]
