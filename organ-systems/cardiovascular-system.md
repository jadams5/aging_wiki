# Cardiovascular System

Navigational overlay for the heart, blood vessels, and their resident cells. The cardiovascular system is among the most aging-burdened body systems: cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of death in older adults, and large-vessel stiffening is one of the most reproducible quantitative biomarkers of vascular age. This page links the atomic entity pages; the claims and citations live on those pages.

## Tissues & organs

- [[heart]] — the muscular pump; age-related diastolic dysfunction, hypertrophy, and conduction changes
- [[myocardium]] — cardiac muscle wall; site of cardiomyocyte loss, fibrosis, and stiffening
- [[arteries]] — elastic and muscular conduit vessels; primary site of stiffening and atherosclerosis
- [[veins]] — capacitance vessels; valve degeneration drives [[chronic-venous-disease]]

## Cell types

- [[cardiomyocytes]] — post-mitotic contractile cells; minimal renewal in adulthood
- [[cardiac-fibroblasts]] — drive [[cardiac-fibrosis]] and extracellular-matrix remodeling
- [[endothelial-cells]] — vascular lining; eNOS/NO decline and endothelial senescence are early events
- [[vsmc]] — vascular smooth muscle; osteogenic transdifferentiation drives [[vascular-calcification]]

## Aging phenotypes

- [[cardiovascular-aging]] — the integrative system-level phenotype
- [[atherosclerosis]] — intimal plaque disease
- [[arterial-stiffening]] — the functional outcome of medial stiffening (pulse-wave velocity)
- [[heart-failure]] — terminal functional decline (HFpEF is the aging-typical form)
- [[cardiac-fibrosis]] — interstitial/perivascular collagen accumulation
- [[chronic-venous-disease]] — venous valve and wall failure
- [[familial-hypercholesterolemia]] — monogenic accelerator of atherosclerosis

## Key processes & axes

- [[vascular-calcification]] — VSMC osteogenic switch; the bone-vascular axis
- The mineral-aging axis intersects here: [[hyperphosphatemia]], [[klotho]], [[fgf23]], [[matrix-gla-protein]], [[vitamin-k]] — phosphate/Klotho dysregulation drives medial calcification
