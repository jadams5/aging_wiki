### Major verifier findings — fabricated/inverted/wrong claims caught

R39 verifier yield exceeded R38 in absolute corrections per page (R38: ~45 across 8 pages = ~5.6/page; R39: ~60 across 12 pages = ~5/page; many higher-impact). Continued the seeder-fabricates-outcomes + seeder-DOI-memory-unreliable patterns from prior memory.

**Critical biology inversion caught:**
- **Krutmann 2017 / Liebmann 2010 red-light claim inverted**: seeder wrote red light (632 nm, 648 nm) + IRA "reduce proliferation and promote keratinocyte differentiation"; paper says they have NO impact — only blue light (412–426 nm) reduces proliferation. Direction was backwards.

**Wholesale primary-source misattributions:**
- **Fisher 2009 MMP-3/MMP-9 elevation claim fabricated**: seeder claimed paper presents quantitative MMP-3 + MMP-9 elevation data; paper has NO such data in main results — MMPs 3/9 appear only in Discussion as possible downstream amplifiers of MMP-1 cleavage products.
