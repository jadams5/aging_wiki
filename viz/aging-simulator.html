Aging causal-graph simulator (v0.3)

Illustrative sensitivity model — parameters are mostly illustrative (see causal-graph-parameters.md). v0.3 — sex-specific competing hazards (CDC WONDER 2022); no sex scalar — female CVD-onset delay & male external excess emerge from data. Mortality sums a per-sex extrinsic channel (accidents / suicide / homicide, scaled by the Lifestyle slider) and a per-sex intrinsic bracket of named CDC causes + a residual, amplified by a sarcopenia-driven frailty multiplier. Coupling propagates intervention relief through the graph as a bounded algebraic fixed point (no integration step-bug). Not prediction, not medical advice.
