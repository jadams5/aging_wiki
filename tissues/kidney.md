# Kidney

#stub — Seeded as a structural anchor for the [[urinary-system]]; not yet written out.

The kidney is the principal organ of phosphate excretion and the main target organ of the bone-mineral endocrine axis: it is where [[fgf23]] (via its [[klotho]] co-receptor) downregulates phosphate reabsorption and suppresses vitamin D activation. Age-related decline in GFR, nephron number, and renal Klotho expression drives [[hyperphosphatemia]] and secondary hyperparathyroidism, tying renal aging to the [[musculoskeletal-system]] and [[cardiovascular-system]]. Renal aging features tubulointerstitial fibrosis, glomerulosclerosis, and a high senescent-cell burden.
