# Thymus

#stub — Seeded as a structural anchor for the [[immune-system]]; not yet written out.

The thymus is the primary lymphoid organ where T-cells mature. **Thymic involution** — the progressive replacement of functional thymic epithelial tissue with fat, beginning in early adulthood — is one of the earliest and most dramatic organ-aging processes and a central mechanistic driver of [[immunosenescence]]: as naïve T-cell output falls, the adaptive repertoire narrows, impairing responses to new antigens and vaccines. Thymic regeneration is an active rejuvenation target.
